# Artifact Management & Master Plans
- Use a strictly enforced 3-tier hierarchy for planning documents:
  1. **Master Plan**: The final, locked-in source of truth. Do NOT edit this directly with new ideas or unconfirmed decisions.
  2. **Staging** (`staging_plan.md`): Accumulates approved findings and decisions from individual discussions. Merge Staging into the Master Plan only when a major planning milestone is reached.
  3. **Discussion** (`discussion_*.md`): Scratchpads for brainstorming specific topics. Never overwrite an active discussion artifact; either append to it, or create a new one for new topics (e.g., `discussion_schema.md`, `discussion_data_flow.md`).
- Workflow: Brainstorm in **Discussion** -> Merge conclusions to **Staging** -> Merge Staging to **Master Plan**.
